On-page Optimization

Optimizing your website pages in order to get a higher rank and more number of traffic in google searches is called as On-page Optimization. Off-page Optimization

It is a technique in which we focus only outside of your website. The main factor to consider in Off–Page Optimization is to create as many high-quality back links as possible to help your website to improve its ranking in search engine pages.
