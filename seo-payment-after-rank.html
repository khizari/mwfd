How It Works

Setup

Google search results show the first ten results on the first page. These top ten positions are divided into three tiers, each with its own daily rate:

Tier 1 1–3
Tier 2 4–5
Tier 3 6–10

After selecting key-phrases for your website, we price per day per keyword according to competition. Positions 1–3 carry the highest daily rate, followed by 4–5, then 6–10.

Example daily pricing for two competitive keywords:

We can set up as many keywords and key-phrases as you'd like.

Billing

Billing

You are billed only when your site appears in the top 10 for the relevant keyword.

Example: if "Business Set up in Dubai" ranks at position 10 for three days in a month, you pay only for those three days — AED 4 × 3 = AED 12.

Your bill is generated at the end of each month based on actual positions held throughout the billing period.

Eligibility

Eligibility

Eligibility depends on the quality of your website — including content, page structure, and source code. We conduct a free audit to determine eligibility before you commit to anything.

If your site doesn't meet the criteria initially, you can still participate by placing a refundable deposit at contract signing. This covers corrections and quality improvements we make to your site. If the contract is terminated early the deposit is not refunded; if honoured in full it is returned at contract end.
